About Us

UberVidz is a video platform that was created in 2020 by software developer and entrepreneur Ivan Mallory. UberVidz allows registered users to upload their own personal videos to the platform for others to view from around the world. Registered users may comment and react to videos as well as follow other users. If you have a video you would like to showcase, become a member today! We look forward to seeing your UberVidz!
